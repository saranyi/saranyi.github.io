Recent Work -

Stored IQ for Legal
The IBM Information Lifecycle Governance portfolio brought in a team of designers mid-2014 to help facilitate portfolio offerings and strategy to produce new, user-friendly offerings. The first of these is Stored IQ for Legal, a comprehensive offering for IBM's previous eDiscovery and Early Case Assessment products.

UX Facilitation and Workshops
All designers have responsibility to give back to the community at large. Workshops and other facilitation-based activities empower both young designers and business leaders to embrace best practices of design thinking in our industry.

artMEAT
artMEAT is an initiative to strengthen the foundation of communities by fostering the collaboration of siloed individual artists, art enthusiasts, creatively inclined, and persons unexposed to the arts. The project is fiscally sponsored by the Austin Creative Alliance.

The Student Publication Volume 36 | Form + Fiction
Form + Fiction looks to engage a discussion on the role of design and designers in shaping, framing, and reflecting reality. While the notion of the designer as a constructor or visionary of a future reality is not a new one, the increasing complexity and connectivity of our world begs for renewed vigor in its evaluation.
