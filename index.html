Shelby Aranyi is an Austin-based designer, artist, and facilitator. Her work aims to foster a spirit of radical collaboration among creatives from diverse backgrounds and disciplines to break down silos in the community. Interests include self-agency, advocacy, and working on kick-ass projects with kooky characters. ◕‿-
Currently ↝
  • a Design Lead/Consultant at IBM Design, working with IBM teams and their clients to evangelize and adopt design thinking within strategy, offerings, engagements, and delivery workflows.

  • the Executive Curator of artMEAT, an initiative aiming to strengthen foundations of the local Austin community by fostering collaboration opportunities between individual artists, art enthusiasts, and persons unexposed to the arts.
